The Valorant program at Esports at Oregon State fields three rosters — Black (Varsity), Orange (JV), and White — competing in CVAL and regional collegiate events. Practices run throughout the week with team-specific schedules, and biweekly inhouses on Saturdays at 8:30 PM give the broader community a chance to play with the comp players.

Whether you're an Immortal-ranked grinder or someone looking to break into organized play for the first time, we have a spot for you. Tryouts open at the start of each fall term — keep an eye on the Discord announcements channel for signups and details.
