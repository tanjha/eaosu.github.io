Who we are

Built by students.
Backed by Beavers.

Esports at Oregon State University has a long history, starting in 2018, we've grown from small to having over 2000 community members. Today we're a registered student organization with competitive rosters in nine titles, a media crew, and a community of hundreds of Beavers who game together every week.

Our competitive teams represent OSU in collegiate leagues across North America — from Riot's official collegiate circuit to NACE Open and the Collegiate Rocket League. Our community arm runs open game nights, intramural-style tournaments, and watch parties all year long.

You don't need to be top-500 to belong here. Whether you're grinding ranked, learning to cast, designing graphics, or just looking for people to queue with after class, there's a seat for you.

How to get involved

Step 01

Join the Discord

Everything runs through our server — announcements, LFG channels, tryout signups, and event RSVPs. Grab your game roles when you land.

Step 02

Show up to a game night

Weekly open sessions, no skill floor. Meet the teams, find your title's community, and play something casual before committing to anything.

Step 03

Try out or staff up

Roster tryouts run at the start of fall term. Not a competitor? Broadcast, design, and event management applications are open year-round.
